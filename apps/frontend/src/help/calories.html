Calories guide

Track food, weight, and diet targets in one workflow.

This page combines nutrition logging, progress review, and planning so daily intake and longer-term goals stay connected.

What this page is for

Calories & Diet supports daily food logging, body-weight tracking, and target planning in one place.

Main tabs

  • Dashboard: review weight trend and food history.
  • Log Food: add food entries.
  • Weight: record new weight logs.
  • Planning: manage the active diet plan.

Important page sections

  • Today's Overview: summarizes calories and macro progress against the target.
  • Weight Trend Chart: shows direction over time.
  • Food Log Table: lists recent entries and allows deletion.

How to use it well

  1. Log food as close to real time as possible.
  2. Check the dashboard to see whether intake matches the plan.
  3. Update weight often enough to make the trend meaningful.
