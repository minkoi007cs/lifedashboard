Habits guide

Track daily consistency and long-term pattern quality.

This page is for recurring behaviors. It helps you manage the list, review recent completion, and understand trend patterns.

What this page is for

Habits are repeatable routines, not one-off tasks. Use this page when the goal is consistency over time.

Main views

  • Daily: manage active habits and mark progress.
  • Heatmap: scan intensity and consistency over time.
  • Analysis: review charts and statistics.

Important elements

  • New Habit: creates a habit from scratch.
  • Edit: updates an existing habit's definition.
  • Quick stats cards: summarize activity and momentum when you are in the daily view.

How to use it well

  1. Keep the habit list small enough to stay honest.
  2. Use the daily view for execution.
  3. Use heatmap and analysis to spot weak days or unrealistic targets.
