Wishlist guide

Turn casual wants into shared planning.

This module helps you store ideas, share them with people in the system, collect reactions, and promote confirmed activities into shared plans.

What this page is for

Wishlist is split into two social sections: what you want, and what other people shared with you. It supports both lightweight sharing and calendar planning.

My Wishlist

  • Create wishes: choose whether the entry is an activity or an item.
  • Time tag: use casual labels like today, this week, or soon.
  • Share: send the wish to selected users in the system.
  • Responses: review confirms, declines, comments, and sharing state.
  • Create plan: available only for activity wishes with valid confirmations.

Friends' Wishes

  • Confirm: say you want to join the activity.
  • Decline: give a negative response if it is not a fit.
  • Comment: add advice or context.
  • Add to plan: if you confirm an activity, you can opt into the shared calendar plan.

Important rules

  • Wishes are private to the owner and the explicitly shared recipients.
  • Responses can be changed before a plan is created.
  • Comment threads continue even after planning.
  • If a wish changes, people who can see it will also see the updated version.
